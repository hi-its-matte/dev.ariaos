Join the Aria Development Team!

We are a passionate team with a mission: to create lightweight, fast, and accessible operating systems for everyone. If you love technology, programming, and want to make a difference, you're in the right place.

Our Vision

We believe that quality software should be available for every type of hardware, from older PCs to modern systems. Our goal is to provide a fluid and intuitive user experience while promoting open source and collaboration.

What We're Looking For developers

We are looking for developers with experience or great enthusiasm in:

  • **Linux Kernel Development:** To optimize performance and hardware compatibility.
  • **Desktop Environment Development (LXQt, GNOME, XFCE, etc.):** To improve the user interface and visual experience.
  • **Scripting (Bash, Python):** For automating installation and configuration processes.
  • **Software Packaging (Debian/Ubuntu):** For managing and integrating applications.
  • **UX/UI Design:** To create intuitive and appealing interfaces.
  • **Technical Documentation:** To write clear guides and manuals for users and other developers.

How to Contribute

There are several ways to join the community and contribute to the Aria project:

  • **Join Our Discord:** Our community is the heart of the project. Discuss ideas, ask for support, and share your knowledge.
  • **Contribute Code:** Our repositories are open. Submit pull requests, report bugs, and help improve the code.
  • **Test Beta Versions:** Your feedback is invaluable for identifying bugs and suggesting improvements before each release.
  • **Create Documentation:** Help make Aria more accessible by writing or improving documentation.
